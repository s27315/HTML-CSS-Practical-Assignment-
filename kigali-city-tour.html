About the City Tour

A Kigali city tour takes you through the history, culture, and modern development of Rwanda's capital. From the Genocide Memorial to the bustling Kimironko Market, there is so much to see and experience.

Top Attractions

Kigali Genocide Memorial

A powerful and moving tribute to the victims of the 1994 genocide. A must-visit for understanding Rwanda's history.

Kimironko Market

One of Kigali's largest and most vibrant markets. Great for local crafts, fresh produce, and authentic Rwandan culture.

Inema Arts Center

A creative hub showcasing contemporary Rwandan art. Visit the gallery, watch live performances, and meet local artists.

Things to Know Before You Go

  • Kigali is one of the safest cities in Africa — walking is generally safe.
  • Moto-taxis are cheap and fast for short distances.
  • Plastic bags are banned — bring a reusable bag.
  • The city is very clean; littering is heavily frowned upon.
  • Most locals speak Kinyarwanda; English is widely understood.
