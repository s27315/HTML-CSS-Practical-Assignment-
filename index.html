Welcome to Kigali

Kigali is the capital and largest city of Rwanda. Known for its cleanliness, safety, and vibrant culture, it is one of Africa's most exciting destinations. Whether you love nature, history, food, or adventure — Kigali has something for everyone.

Top Travel Tips

  1. Carry your passport or national ID at all times.
  2. Use Moto-taxis (motorcycle taxis) for short trips.
  3. Try local dishes like Ugali, Isombe, and Brochettes.
  4. Respect the no-plastic-bag rule in Rwanda.
  5. Visit the Genocide Memorial to understand Rwanda's history.
