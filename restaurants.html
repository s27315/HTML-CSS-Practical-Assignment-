Eat Well in Kigali

Kigali's food scene is diverse and growing fast. From traditional Rwandan cuisine to international flavours, the city offers something for every taste and budget. Here are some of the top spots you should not miss.

Top Restaurants

Heaven Restaurant

🌍 International | $$$

One of Kigali's most celebrated restaurants, offering stunning views and a menu blending Rwandan and international cuisine.

Repub Lounge

🍖 Local & Grills | $$

A popular spot for brochettes, grilled meats, and cold drinks. Great atmosphere with live music on weekends.

Zen Restaurant

🍜 Asian Fusion | $$

Serving authentic Asian dishes in a calm, elegant setting. Known for its sushi, noodles, and friendly service.

Meze Fresh

🥗 Healthy & Fresh | $

A go-to for healthy wraps, salads, and fresh juices. Perfect for a quick and nutritious lunch in the city.

Must-Try Local Dishes

  • Ugali – A stiff maize porridge served with stews or vegetables.
  • Isombe – Cassava leaves cooked with eggplant and spinach.
  • Brochettes – Grilled meat skewers, a Rwandan street food favourite.
  • Ibirayi – Fried or boiled potatoes, often served as a side dish.
  • Akabanga – Rwanda's famous chilli oil, used to spice up any meal.
